Hi! My name is Pei-Chi Lo (羅珮綺). I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Information Systems at National Sun Yat-sen University. Previously, I obtained my PhD in Computer Science under the supervision of Professor Ee-Peng Lim at Singapore Management University (SMU).

You are welcome to join my lab if you are also interested in information retrieval, computational linguistics, or anything exciting in related fields.
If you want to know more about me, here's my  CV  .
Also, feel free to contact me at pclo [at] mis.nsysu.edu.tw
